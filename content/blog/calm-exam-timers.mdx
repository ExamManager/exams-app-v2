Exam halls need clocks that staff can trust at a glance. ExamManager V2 was built around a large classroom timer: clear digits, obvious status, and controls that stay quiet until an invigilator needs pause or extra time.

This post is part of the **ExamManager V2 portfolio showcase**. Auth and billing are disabled — the product story remains for visitors browsing the public demos.

## Design principles

- **Readable from the desk** — type and contrast suited to a busy room, not a marketing landing page.
- **One primary action** — start, pause, and finish should never compete with decorative chrome.
- **Student view option** — when projecting, show only what candidates need to see.
