Exam weeks fail in the logistics, not the curriculum. ExamManager V2 grouped planning tools — timetables, seating, and multi-paper sessions — so staff could prepare ahead and run the day from one place.

This article belongs to a **deprecated portfolio demo**. Plans on the [pricing page](/pricing) are illustrative only.

## What “good enough” planning looked like

1. Confirm every paper’s duration and room the day before.
2. Build seating so candidates know where to go before the timer starts.
3. Group overlapping papers when one invigilator covers multiple cohorts.
4. Launch timers from the session list instead of rewriting times under pressure.
