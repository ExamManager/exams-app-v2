ExamManager V2 is a **deprecated portfolio demo**. It is not an active product and does not offer live accounts or billing on this deployment.

## What this site does

This public site shows marketing pages, documentation, and illustrative pricing. Authentication endpoints are disabled. You should not expect personal account data to be collected or stored through signup flows here.

## Contact

For questions about this portfolio project, use the [ExamManager GitHub organization](https://github.com/ExamManager). There is no support inbox for this demo.
